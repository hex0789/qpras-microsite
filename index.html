About

Professor Hector Ortiz Jr., physicist and AI researcher, introduces QPRAS — a pioneering system that explores the intersection of quantum programming, decision theory, and multiverse-informed influence. This initiative challenges the boundaries of what is possible, using probabilistic nudging and ethically constrained quantum modeling to reveal paths toward unprecedented human potential.

The Vision

The ultimate goal is not to control outcomes, but to align them — increasing the likelihood of globally beneficial decisions across high-impact individuals and systems. QPRAS seeks to become the cognitive bridge between complex probabilistic futures and human intention.
